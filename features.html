List of Features of CyberTooth

Embrace the POWER of Digitalizing your Clinic

  1. Dashboard and analytics

    This feature allows for real-time monitoring of clinic performance, appointment scheduling, and patient data, which can aid in decision-making and strategic planning.

  2. Appointment scheduling

    This feature allows patients to schedule appointments and for the clinic to manage and track their schedule.

  3. Electronic health records (EHR)

    This feature allows for the storage, management, and sharing of patient health information.

  4. Treatment planning

    This feature allows for the creation and management of treatment plans for patients, including estimates for cost and duration of treatment.

  5. Marketing and patient acquisition

    This feature allows for patient acquisition strategies, including the ability to track patient engagement and conversion.

  6. Reporting and analytics

    This feature allows for the generation of various reports and analytics on patient demographics, treatment statistics, and financial performance.

  7. Drugs management

    This feature allows for the ability to track drugs as needed.

  8. Payments management

    This feature allows for the tracking and management of patient payments, including the ability to process payments and track outstanding balances.

  9. Price lists

    This feature allows for the management of the clinic's pricing structure, including the ability to create different price lists for different services and insurance plans.

  10. Visits management

    This feature allows for the tracking and management of patient visits, including the ability to document patient complaints and treatment outcomes.

  11. Labs management

    This feature allows for the tracking and management of lab orders, and integrates them with patient records.

  12. Expense management

    This feature allows for the tracking and management of clinic expenses, including the ability to track spending, and generate financial reports.

  13. Billing and insurance management

    This feature allows for the management of patient billing and insurance information, including claims processing and payments.

  14. Mobile access

    This feature allows patients and staff to access the software via mobile devices, which is helpful in case of emergency or when they are not in the clinic.

  15. Access control or Roles & Permissions

    This feature allows for the management of user access, which can be used to ensure that only authorized personnel can access sensitive patient information.

  16. Secure access

    This feature allows authorized personnel to access patient information and manage the clinic's operations while maintaining the security and confidentiality of patient data.

  17. Backup and disaster recovery

    This feature allows for the backup and recovery of data in case of system failure or data loss, ensuring the data is safe and can be restored in case of an emergency.

  18. Cloud-based storage

    This feature allows for the storage of patient data and other clinic information on cloud-based servers, providing a secure and scalable solution for data management.

  19. Multilingual support

    This feature allows for the software to be used in different languages, which can be beneficial for clinics with English as well as Arabic speakers.

  20. Scalability

    This feature allows for the software to be easily scaled to accommodate the clinic's growth, which can be beneficial for clinics that are expanding.

  21. Search and filter functions

    This feature allows for the easy searching and filtering of patient records, which can be useful for quickly accessing the information needed.

  22. Doctor's percentage calculation

    This feature allows for the calculation of the percentage of revenue that each doctor generates, which can be used for determining compensation and tracking performance.

  23. Digital X-ray management

    This feature allows for the storage and management of digital X-rays, which can be easily accessed and shared with other healthcare providers as needed.

  24. Imaging management

    This feature allows for the storage and management of other types of imaging such as CT, MRI, and cone beam, which can be easily accessed and shared with other healthcare providers as needed.

  25. Document management

    This feature allows for the storage and management of other types of documents such as prescriptions, lab results, and insurance information, which can be easily accessed and shared with other healthcare providers as needed.

  26. Digital consent management

    This feature allows for the storage and management of consent forms digitally, which can be easily accessed, shared and retrieved as needed.

  27. User-friendly interface

    This feature allows for an intuitive and easy-to-use interface, which can be beneficial for staff members who are not familiar with the software.

  28. Training and support

    This feature provides training and support resources, such as tutorials, help guides, and customer support, which can be useful for staff who are new to the software or who need assistance.
